OUR DEPARTMENTS

Our School is divided into 3 Main Departments namely:

1. KINDERGARTEN SECTION

We wish to welcome you all to Qubaa Nursery School. Under the new management we care and take care of all children regardless of their race and tribe.


Qubaa Nursery is big and can accommodate 200 children in it. The classes are attractive and spacious where children can learn through, play, sing e.t.c. We have a beautiful play room with attractive playing materials, smart boards, computer classes and out door materials;-swings, see-saw.


The teachers are qualified, experienced, energetic and flexible in all their duties. For quality education come to Qubaa Muslim Nursery School.

2. PRIMARY SECTION

Qubaa Muslim Primary School is a well established school offering the best quality educational services in the most conducive environment. The school uses leaner centred approach in all its programmes.


This has enabled our institution to produce primary graduates who go ahead to excel in their secondary education. Thus joining various universities afterwards.


The students also have wider knowledge and effective written and oral communication skills. A strong foundation in bringing up a holistic child.

3. SECONDARY SECTION

The school opened its doors as an educational institution of higher leaning in 1996. It offers 8.4.4. Kenyan education system. It is a mixed school where both boys and girls have equal chances to education. However, the school practises gender separation policy whereby there are separate classes for the boys and for the girls.


The present principal is Mr. Lennox Mtengo who took over from the late Mr matran in January 2013. Under the new management and administration the school is now undergoing a positive radical transformation.


We have highly qualified, experienced and motivated teachers. The students disciplined and ready to learn at all times

OFFICE OF THE DEAN

The office coordinates the academic activities of the school. The departments under this section include:

  1. Mathematics department.
  2. Science department:Chemistry, biology, physics.
  3. Language department: English, Kiswahili, Arabic.
  4. Technical department: Home science, Computer studies, Business studies.
  5. Humanity department: History, CRE, IRE, Geography.

SUBJECT SELECTION

EDUCATIONAL TRIPS AND EXCURSSIONS:

For learnig is a matter of hearing and seeing,we frequently organise educational trips and excurssions based on subjects where the students go and see practically what they learn in class for more familiarisation with the subject.


All this is aimed at inspiring the students, discouraging class monotony, motivating and encourages the learner.They are well organised and planned by the subject teachers on consultation with the H.O.D and the principal/management.All trips organised have been very successful on their objectives.
