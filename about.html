BRIEF HISTORY OF QUBAA MUSLIM SCHOOL


(from 14-1-1987)

The school started in the premises of Qubaa mosque in majengo near MAJENGO sokoni in Mombasa after which the school was named QUBAA PRIMARY SCHOOL. In January, 14th 1987 recruitment of pupils started and the school opened its doors as a government aided school having established four classes namely B1, G1,B2 and G2.
