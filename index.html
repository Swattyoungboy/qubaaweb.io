SCHOOL EVENTS

Qubaa Muslim school offers a variety of events to keep the students both mentally and physically active. Such activities help develop social skills and empathyâ€”the outward-oriented dimensions of emotional intelligence (EQ). The interactions or conversations elicited by events helps students build relationships, understand different perspectives and engage other cultures.

Graduation

Kindergarten 3 pupils are promoted to the primary section with a colorful graduation ceremony and awarding of certificates. It is normally done in the third term. This is also the case for form four students who have completed their final year examinations

Fun & Art Day

Qubaa muslim school fun & art day is organized by the pre-primary section. Children from various schools in the county get a chance to participate in various activities like painting. The children are then judged and awarded accordingly.

Cultural Day

There was an inter-cultural music competition in which different cultures were exhibited, the competition was a big success and a big boost to cultural presentation.

Academic Contest

We hold annual academic contests for class seven and eight pupils. This brings together all the best pupils from the region to test their academic powers. This motivates the learners to do even better in a bid to excel. Medals and Gifts are normally awarded to the winners.

Quran Competition

The Quraan recitation contest is normally held during the month of Ramadhan. It is intended to motivate the learners to continue reading and treasuring the Quraan and its valuable teachings.

Sports Day

As per the school tradition, sports inter-house competition is conducted annually in May. The students get a chance to discover and exploit their talents during this competitive event. The best performers and the best houses are awarded accordingly.

Quiz & Debate Competitions

The learners are regularly trained on confidence, quick reasoning and language command through internal quick fire quizzes and hot debates.

Academic Open Day

The academic open day is normally held in the third academic session. The learners get a chance to exhibit the best of what they have learnt in class.

School Trips

The Students are taken for school trips once in a term to where place chosen by both the teacher and the students at large. These trips prove to be both fun and also educational
